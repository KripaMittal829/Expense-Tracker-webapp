This is a basic expense tracker web app designed especially for beginners who want a simple and effective way to manage their daily finances. The app focuses on ease of use rather than complex features, making it ideal for users with little to no experience in financial tracking.

The interface follows a clean green and white color theme, giving it a fresh and minimal look. The design is straightforward, with clear sections and easy navigation so users can quickly understand how to use the app without any learning curve. There are no unnecessary elements, which helps keep the experience smooth and distraction-free.

Users can easily add their expenses by entering simple details such as the amount and a short description. All transactions are displayed in an organized list, allowing users to view and manage their spending in one place. The app also provides a basic summary of total expenses, helping users keep track of how much they are spending over time.

This expense tracker does not overwhelm users with advanced tools or complicated analytics. Instead, it focuses on the core purpose—recording and monitoring expenses in the simplest way possible. This makes it especially useful for students or anyone just starting to build the habit of managing money.

Additionally, the app is designed to work smoothly across different devices, ensuring accessibility and convenience. Overall, it offers a clean, beginner-friendly solution for staying organized and developing better financial habits without any complexity.
